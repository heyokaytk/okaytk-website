After years of letting the music speak for itself, I figured it was time to put some words down too.

## What This Is

This blog is where I'll write about the craft — DJ culture, event recaps, gear I'm using, music I can't stop playing, and whatever else feels worth saying.

## What's Coming

Expect posts on:

- Event breakdowns and setlists
- How I build a set for different crowds
- Halifax's music scene and where it's headed
- The business side of freelance DJing

> "The DJ is the last living medium between the artist and the crowd."

Not every post will be a long read. Some will be short. All of them will be honest.
