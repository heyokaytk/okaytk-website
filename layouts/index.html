ABOUT OKAY TK:

OKAY TK is a Canadian DJ and event curator built on competition floors, packed rooms, and consistent execution at the highest level. A multiple-time Red Bull 3Style National Finalist, he's known for technical precision, seamless transitions, and an open-format approach driven by the love of music and musical intent over gimmicks. His work includes his personal edits, national television, brand partnerships with Red Bull, Nike, Ford, and Corona, and performances across North America—from Vancouver's Commodore Ballroom to Austin's Inn Cahoots—plus festival stages and including a couple JUNO performances. Whether in clubs, festivals, corporate settings, or online, OKAY TK delivers a disciplined, culture-first approach focused on connection over spectacle.
