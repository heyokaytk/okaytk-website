About

OKAY TK is an acclaimed DJ from Halifax, Nova Scotia, with over 20+ years of experience. Known for his stage presence, song selection, and crowd pleasing performances, OKAY TK has made his mark across Canada. His talent has been recognized, and has had the privilege of sharing the stage with some of your favourite artists, DJs, and producers. OKAY TK has also represented top corporate clients, including Nike, Dash Hudson, Ford, Red Bull Canada, TD, and The Juno Awards.
